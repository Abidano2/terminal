Hey! As you can tell by the progress bar, progress has been made.

If you couldn't tell already, yes this is a game. This game currently doesn't have a name so I'll be calling it Project Atlas for now. It will be divided in "Chapters" with each one being more than an hour long.

I have a team helping with this including 2 other story writers and an artist, the rest is being handled by me.

Almost half of Chapter 1 has been written. After I'm done writing it we'll start writing multiple chapters in parallel with me writing Chapter 2 and the other writers writing Chapters 3 and 4, with me helping of course. After Chapter 1 is finished I'd like to release it as a demo, I don't know when that will happen though. I'd also like to expand the team a little more by getting a musician and a second programmer.
